## Logic kya hai?

**Logic** reasoning ka systematic study hai. Yeh humein sikhata hai ki valid conclusions kaise derive karein.

### Statement (Kathan)

Ek **statement** woh sentence hai jo ya to true hai ya false.

**Examples:**
- "Earth gol hai" — yeh ek statement hai (true)
- "2 + 2 = 5" — yeh ek statement hai (false)
- "Kya tum theek ho?" — yeh statement **nahi** hai (yeh question hai)

### Truth Values

Har statement ka ek **truth value** hota hai:
- **True (T)** — jab statement sahi ho
- **False (F)** — jab statement galat ho

Mathematical notation mein, agar $p$ ek statement hai, to:

$$p \in \{T, F\}$$

---

## Examples

Neeche diye gaye mein se kaun statements hain?

1. "Sun purab mein rise hota hai"
2. "Please sit down"
3. "$\sqrt{2}$ ek irrational number hai"

**Answer:** 1 aur 3 statements hain.
